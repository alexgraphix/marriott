MEET & BE REWARDED (the “Promotion”) TERMS & CONDITIONS

  • If a customer (the “Qualified Member” see definition of “Qualified Member” below) books a meeting or an event at a Participating Property* located in Asia Pacific between 1 October 2018 and 31 December 2018, that has an arrival date on or before 31 December, 2019, with a minimum of ten (10) guest rooms booked on the peak night (the “Qualifying Event”), and satisfies the terms and conditions of this Promotion:
  • the Customer that enters in an agreement with the Participating Property for the Qualifying Event (the “Qualifying Event Agreement”) may enjoy the following benefits (subject to availability at the Participating Property, and no substitutions or credits will be permitted):
    1. the Customer that enters into the Qualifying Event Agreement with the Participating Property for the Qualifying Event will have the total contracted room night charges on its master bill for the Qualifying Event reduced by a 5% discount;
    2. Two (2) bonus points for every US$1.00 of eligible revenue will be posted to the Qualified Member’s account within 6–8 weeks of the participating Marriott Rewards®, The Ritz-Carlton Rewards® and SPG® hotel’s posting of the base points, up to a maximum of 60,000 bonus points. The bonus points are in addition to base earnings for Qualifying Event Agreements per the Marriott Rewarding Events terms and conditions. Combined earned and bonus points may never exceed 200,000 points;
    3. One complimentary one-hour welcome reception hosted by the Participating Property in a space chosen at the discretion of the Participating Property for the number of guests equal to the number of guest rooms actualized on the peak night of the Qualifying Event up to a maximum of 500 guests. The welcome reception will include complimentary assorted hors d’oeuvres (3 pieces per person), plus beer/wine/sodas for one-hour. After such one-hour period has lapsed, menu items and beverages may be purchased by “Qualified Member” at prevailing menu prices. Reception food and beverage item selections are at the discretion of the hotel and will be outlined in the Qualifying Event Agreement. Not valid at hotels with third party food vendors. Normal rates will apply for additional reception attendees. The location of the welcome reception space is at the discretion of the Participating Property.

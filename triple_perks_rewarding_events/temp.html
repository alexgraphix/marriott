  • If a customer (the “Qualified Member” see definition of “Qualified Member” below) books a meeting or an event at a Participating Property* located in Asia Pacific between 1 October 2018 and 31 May 2019, that has an arrival date on or before 31 May 2019, with a maximum of nine (9) guest rooms booked on the peak night (the “Qualifying Event”), and satisfies the terms and conditions of this promotion:
  • the Customer that enters in an agreement with the Participating Property for the Qualifying Event (the “Qualifying Event Agreement”) may enjoy one (1) of the following benefits (subject to availability at the Participating Property, and no substitutions or credits will be permitted):

    2. Two (2) bonus points for every US$1.00 of eligible revenue will be posted to the Qualified Member’s account within 6–8 weeks of the participating Marriott Rewards®, The Ritz-Carlton Rewards® and SPG® hotel’s posting of the base points, up to a maximum of 60,000 bonus points. Combined earned and bonus points may never exceed 200,000 points.

One complimentary one-hour welcome drinks/ beverage reception hosted by the Participating Property in a space chosen at the discretion of the Participating Property for the total number of meeting participants up to a maximum of 500 guests. The welcome drinks/ beverage reception will include beer/wine/sodas for one hour. After such one-hour period has lapsed, beverages may be purchased by Customer at prevailing menu prices. Beverage items are chosen at the discretion of the Participating Property and will be outlined in the Qualifying Event Agreement. Not valid at Participating Properties with third party food/ beverage vendors. Normal rates will apply for additional reception attendees. The welcome drinks reception space is chosen at the discretion of the Participating Property.
